About

Strategy that starts with people

I'm Pieter Van den Keybus, a CX and UX strategist based in Antwerp, Belgium. Through my consultancy Uflow, I help organisations design user-friendly digital products and customer-centred services.

With over ten years of experience in UX research, service design and digital strategy, I work across a diverse portfolio — from Belgian companies to international organisations.

My approach combines qualitative user research with strategic insight — always focused on concrete, actionable recommendations that make a real difference for users and organisations alike.
